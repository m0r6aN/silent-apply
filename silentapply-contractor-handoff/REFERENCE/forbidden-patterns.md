# Forbidden Patterns

The following patterns violate SilentApply doctrine and must not be used.

## Motion and Interaction

- Spinners or loading indicators
- Progress bars or percentages
- Step counters
- Success animations or celebratory effects
- Checkmark bounces
- Fast hover effects
- Scroll-jacking or forced scroll behavior
- Parallax exaggeration
- Rhythmic acceleration of motion
- Motion synced to user actions for emphasis
- Bounce, elastic, overshoot, or snap easing
- Animated entrances for primary content
- Auto-scroll or auto-advance behaviors

## Visual and Content

- Non-canonical visuals or marks
- New icons or symbol sets
- People, resumes, or job metaphors
- Embedded text in hero imagery
- Persuasive, urgent, or clever tone

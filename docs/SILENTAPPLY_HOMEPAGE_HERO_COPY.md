## Common Principles Across Variations

1. **Lead with Product**: SilentApply AI is always the primary subject
2. **Imply Hierarchy**: Reference OMEGA and Keon without explaining their relationship
3. **Focus on Outcomes**: Emphasize interview wins, time savings, and reduced friction
4. **Avoid AI Explanation**: Assume audience understands AI as infrastructure for opportunity
5. **Governance as Trust**: Position boundaries as enabling candidate comfort and privacy

## Tone Notes

* **Variation 1**: Direct, benefit-forward for candidates
* **Variation 2**: Conceptual, intelligence-focused for tech audience
* **Variation 3**: Practical, efficiency-oriented for recruiters

All variations maintain the restrained, confident tone established in the Brand Doctrine while speaking to different audience segments.

## Audience-Specific Messaging

### For Candidates
- Focus on time savings and interview conversion
- Emphasize control over privacy and visibility
- Highlight the "single link" concept

### For Recruiters
- Focus on efficiency and quality of information
- Emphasize reduced administrative overhead
- Highlight instant answers and scheduling

### For Enterprise
- Focus on compliance, security, and integration
- Emphasize systematic improvement over point solutions
- Highlight governed intelligence

## Common Principles Across Variations

1. **Lead with Product**: ForgePilot AI is always the primary subject
2. **Imply Hierarchy**: Reference OMEGA and Keon without explaining their relationship
3. **Focus on Outcomes**: Emphasize transformation, execution, and results
4. **Avoid AI Explanation**: Assume audience understands AI as infrastructure
5. **Governance as Feature**: Position boundaries as enabling rather than limiting
